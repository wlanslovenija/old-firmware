odprto brezžično omrežje Slovenije

Dobrodošel, dobrodošla!

Povezuješ se na odprto brezžično omrežje Slovenije, kjer se z uporabo splošno razširjenih brezžičnih tehnologij, a z inovativnim pristopom in medsebojnim sodelovanjem povezujemo v samostojno in neodvisno brezžično omrežje nad Slovenijo.

Omrežje je rezultat pobude izgradnje skupnega odprtega brezžičnega omrežja in pri tem lahko sodeluješ ter pomagaš tudi ti. Več o tem na domači strani omrežja.

Welcome!

You are connecting to the open wireless network of Slovenia where we with the use of common and widespread wireless technology but with the innovative approach and collaboration connect into an autonomous and independent wireless network over Slovenia.

Network is the result of the initiative to build a common open wireless network. You can also take part and help. More about that on the network's homepage.

Z vstopom v omrežje (vsaj začasno) postaneš del omrežja in skupnosti okoli njega. Uporabljaj ga s čutom za skupno dobro.

By entering into the network you (at least temporarily) become part of the network and community around it. Use it with a touch for the common good.

Pozor: Zaradi odprte brezžične narave omrežja podatki, ki se po njem pretakajo, niso zaščiteni. Zato priporočamo uporabo varnih SSL/TLS povezav do spletnih strani (ki se jih prepozna po HTTPS predponi naslova) in drugih storitev. Prav tako priporočamo vklop požarnega zidu na računalniku v airport (ali podobnem) načinu, ki ne dovoljuje izjem požarnega zidu.

Warning: Because of the network's open wireless nature data transmitted over it is not protected. We recommend usage of secure SSL/TLS connections to web pages (which are recognizable by HTTPS address prefix) and other services. Furthermore, we recommend to activate firewall on the computer in airport (or similar) mode, which does not allow firewall exceptions.
